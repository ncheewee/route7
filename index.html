Good to know

🪪
White plate scooter is fine on Route 7 and Route 9 — both are regular provincial highways (省道), no restriction. It's only expressways/national freeways (國道) that are off-limits, which is why Friday's return no longer uses Freeway 5.
🚧
Check road status before you leave both mornings. Route 7 has recurring rockfall closures (Yingshi, Niudou, Minchi) after rain, plus a standing night closure on the Daman section (18:00–08:00). Live status: 168.thb.gov.tw
🌧️
Afternoon storms are the norm. Typical summer pattern peaks early-mid afternoon. Aim to clear exposed mountain sections before 13:00–14:00 both days. Shelter points marked green in the timeline.
⚠️
Wet hairpins > wet climbs. If caught in rain, prioritize waiting it out over riding a hairpin descent wet — Thursday's 九彎十八拐 and Friday's Pinglin descent are both worth this caution.
🎒
Pack: rain shell/poncho, anti-fog visor treatment, IDP + Singapore licence + passport, cash for rental deposit.
